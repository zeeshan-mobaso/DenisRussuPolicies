Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play?
A: Swipe or use arrow keys to shift rows and columns. The grid wraps around — your goal is to match the target pattern shown on the right.

Q: Does the game require an internet connection?
A: No. The entire game works offline.

Q: Can I reset or start a new puzzle?
A: Yes! Tap the “New” or “Reset” buttons anytime to begin again.
